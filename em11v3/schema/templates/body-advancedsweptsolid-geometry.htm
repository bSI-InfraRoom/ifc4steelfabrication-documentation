4.4.3.1.3 Body AdvancedSweptSolid Geometry

Swept Disc Solid shall only be used for non straight pipe, which may show up in the factory fabricated stair railing. Bought-Out railing may use Boundary Representation for its geometrical representation if needed. Straight pipe should use Extruded Area Solid with Circle Hollow Profile.
