5.2.4.27 AISC_EM11_Pset_TubeOffshoreChamfer

Tube Offshore Chamfer: An offshore chamfer can be compared with a normal chamfer, except that in the heel zone the fit line of the macro has been transformed to the outside of the cut pipe. It is called offshore chamfer as this chamfer is widely used in the offshore industry. Most of these macros comply with the standards set in the American Welding Standard (AWS).
