5.2.4.26 AISC_EM11_Pset_TubeOffshoreSaddle

Tube Offshore Saddle: An offshore saddle can be compared with a normal saddle, except that in the heel zone the fit line of the profile has been transformed to the outside of the cut pipe. It is called offshore saddle as this saddle is widely used in the offshore. Most of these profiles comply with the standards set in the American Welding Standard (AWS).
